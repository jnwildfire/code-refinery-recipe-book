# Paradise Cream
## Ingredients
- 1 cup milk
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 cup butter
- 1 cup flour
- 1 cup cocoa powder
- 1 cup vanilla extract
- 1 cup chocolate chips

## Instructions
1. In a large saucepan, combine the milk, sugar, and butter. Heat over medium heat until the butter is melted and the mixture is smooth.
2. Add the flour,
3. Add the cocoa powder,
4. Add the vanilla extract,
5. Add the chocolate chips,
6. Continue to cook, stirring constantly, until the mixture thickens and comes to a boil. This should take about 5-7 minutes.
7. Pour the cream into a bowl and chill in the fridge for at least 2 hours.
8. Serve with ice cream.
