# Brownie
## Ingredients
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 cup butter
- 1 cup flour
- 1 cup milk

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
2. Mix sugar, butter, flour, and milk in a bowl until smooth.
3. Pour into a 9x13-inch baking dish.
4. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
5. Let cool before cutting into squares and serving. Enjoy!
